How to play

Reach the green exit. Every wall is a mirror.

Move: left stick (or W A S D)

Look: right stick, or drag the view (or arrow keys)

Hints: glowing gems hide in dead ends — touch one to light the way out for a few seconds.

Keys: Space starts · R gives up · Esc restarts
